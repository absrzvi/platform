## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Single-question modal that fires when a disruption is detected — gives Stefan a half-second beat to confirm switching from triage scan to crisis-handling mode. The modal does *not* present the crisis itself; it confirms intent to enter crisis state.

**User Situation:** Stefan is in triage view (01.1). A new disruption fires upstream (e.g. door motor fault, Unit 4722). The modal appears centred over his current view with the underlying view dimmed (not blurred — Stefan must still see the rest of the fleet).

**Success Criteria:**
- Modal appears within 500ms of disruption event reaching client
- Stefan can confirm with mouse OR keyboard (Enter / Esc)
- Esc dismisses without entering crisis state; the card remains in the triage feed at appropriate severity

**Entry Points:**
- Push event from `/api/intentpackets/stream` matching crisis severity threshold

**Exit Points:**
- "View" → [01.3-crisis-fullwidth](../01.3-crisis-fullwidth/01.3-crisis-fullwidth.md)
- Dismiss (Esc, "Not now") → returns to [01.1-triage-view](../01.1-triage-view/01.1-triage-view.md); card still in feed

## Technical Notes

- **Modal library:** base-ui Dialog with `role=alertdialog`, focus-trapped.
- **Auto-focus:** primary action ("View") gets focus on open.
- **No queuing UX:** if a second crisis-severity event fires while the modal is open, it does NOT replace or stack — it joins the triage feed as a new card and Stefan handles it after the current modal closes.
- **Auto-dismiss on upstream resolution:** subscribe to `/api/intentpackets/stream` for the same `intent_id`; if status becomes "resolved" before Stefan acts, close modal silently and show toast.
- **Severity threshold for modal firing:** PRD defines crisis-modal trigger as CRITICAL severity with `request_landside_tasking: true`. Other severities surface in triage only (no modal).

## Open Questions

| # | Question | Context | Status |
|---|----------|---------|--------|
| 1 | Modal timing — does the modal stay until Stefan acts, or auto-dismiss after N seconds? | Stefan persona F3: "co-pilot must not be slower than HAFAS." A modal that auto-dismisses might be missed; one that lingers might block him from other work. Suggest: no auto-dismiss, but show timestamp ("Fired 8s ago"). | 🔴 Open |
| 2 | Is the modal blocking, or can Stefan interact with triage behind it? | Tradeoff: modality forces attention but disrupts flow. Suggest: blocking (focus trap), with a "Not now" that defers without losing the card. | 🟡 In Discussion |
| 3 | Should the modal preview cost-impact one-liner before Stefan clicks View? | Earlier cost visibility — but extra modal real estate. | 🔴 Open |

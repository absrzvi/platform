## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Klaus drills into a WARNING fault card to see the deferral story — what was deferred, by whom, why, and when it should next be addressed. WARNING cards typically end in a DEFER rather than a resolution; the card detail tells Klaus the deferral is legitimate (or surfaces if it isn't) so he can decide whether to clear the vehicle for service anyway.

**User Situation:** Klaus saw the WARNING card on the brief (03.2). He taps it to verify the deferral has a reason and an owner.

**Success Criteria:**
- Defer reason visible prominently — the reason text is what Klaus uses to make his clearance judgment
- Defer attribution clear — who deferred, when, with which reason category
- Service-impact statement makes clear the deferral is non-blocking
- Next-action surface — when is this expected to be addressed (next maintenance window, next G-Check, etc.)

**Entry Points:**
- Tap on WARNING (or INFO) card in [03.2-prearrival-brief](../03.2-prearrival-brief/03.2-prearrival-brief.md)

**Exit Points:**
- Collapse → returns to brief feed
- Tap "Record sign-off" (header CTA) → [03.5-return-record-entry](../03.5-return-record-entry/03.5-return-record-entry.md)

## Page States

Same as 03.3 + the following warning-specific states:

| State | When | Appearance | Actions |
|-------|------|------------|---------|
| Defer reason missing | DEFER action was logged but reason field empty (shouldn't happen by Elena's 04.7 spec, but defensive) | Reason text section shows "Reason text not provided" with a warning indicator; Klaus can still proceed but the audit row will flag this | Collapse, navigate, surface to ops |
| Expected window past | Expected resolution window has already passed | Window line shows in warning treatment: "Expected resolution: Was scheduled {time} (past — escalate)" | Same actions; consider escalation |
| Cross-tier (was WARNING, became CRITICAL) | Fault re-classified between brief generation and Klaus's review | Card visibly re-tags from WARNING to CRITICAL; toast notifies; Klaus re-reviews | Same actions |

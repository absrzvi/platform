## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Klaus has walked to Bay 3, confirmed with the technician, signed paper at 06:08. He returns to the PWA and taps "Record sign-off" on the brief card for Unit 4722. This surface explicitly frames the shadow record as a *companion to paper*, not as the sign-off itself. It is the consent step before the hold-to-record gesture fires.

This is the **regulatory contract surface** of the platform. Every word matters.

**User Situation:** Klaus has the brief open from 03.4. He's standing in the bay or just outside it. Paper is signed. He needs to log the shadow record. He taps "Record sign-off" on the brief.

**Success Criteria:**
- The advisory-framing is explicit and non-removable: "platform shadow, paper authoritative"
- Klaus reads ≤2 short sentences before the hold gesture is available
- No timers, no countdowns, no urgency cues — Klaus controls the pace (per persona F2)
- Vehicle + sign-off context restated so Klaus is sure he's recording the right thing
- Cancel is always one tap away

**Entry Points:**
- "Record sign-off" affordance from [03.4-card-detail-warning](../03.4-card-detail-warning/) (any card in the brief that's resolution-complete and ready for sign-off)

**Exit Points:**
- Klaus initiates hold gesture → [03.6-hold-to-record](../03.6-hold-to-record/03.6-hold-to-record.md)
- Klaus cancels → returns to brief view (no audit row written)

## Technical Notes

- **Vehicle context server-rendered:** the modal opens with vehicle + bay + paper-time pre-populated from the brief card's current state. No re-fetching here; if the brief is stale, the brief view itself surfaces the staleness (out of scope for this page).
- **Paper time editable on brief card:** Klaus enters his paper sign-off time on the brief card (default = device time, editable). This page renders that value; if Klaus realises the time is wrong here, he cancels and corrects on the brief.
- **Identity check before hold:** when Klaus taps hold-to-record, the client verifies the IdP token is fresh. If expired, the modal pivots to the "Insufficient identity" state and offers biometric re-auth in-place.
- **Offline support:** the audit row write queues locally (IndexedDB) when offline; success state surfaces a "Queued" status. On reconnect, the queued write fires with the original idempotency key.
- **No haptic on countdown initiation:** persona F2 — no pressure cues. Haptic only on touch-start (signalling "your touch is registered") and at success (signalling completion). No haptic during the 3s hold or the 5s countdown.

| 2 | Should the modal show a thumbnail of the resolution chain so Klaus visually re-confirms? | Pro: defensive against wrong-unit recording. Con: adds visual weight to the regulatory surface. Recommend: omit on this page (Klaus has just been on the brief); add a "View resolution chain" link as a secondary affordance. | 🔴 Open |
| 3 | German translation for the LOCKED framing paragraphs | Pre-pilot blocker for German ÖBB context. Recommend: get translation from ÖBB legal + ECM owners (Renate / Martin) at first wds-0 alignment session. | 🔴 Open |
| 4 | Network-offline state — should the "Queued, will sync on reconnect" message persist on the success state visible to Klaus on next sync? | Yes — until Klaus has seen the queued sign-off match against the paper (Renate's audit committee will ask), the audit row is in a "pre-reconciliation" state. | 🟢 Resolved: persistent queued indicator until reconciled |

| 6 | Reinforcement line — is "Confirm with technician on site before recording" universal, or context-dependent? | The line addresses Klaus's persona F4; it's generic enough for any sign-off. Recommend: lock as universal MVP copy. | 🟢 Resolved: universal MVP |
